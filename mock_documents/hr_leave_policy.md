# HR Leave Policy

## Purpose

Community Support Organization provides a clear process for staff leave requests while protecting private employee information.

## Requesting Leave

Staff should submit planned leave requests through the approved internal HR process as early as possible. The request should include the dates requested, type of leave, and any scheduling notes needed for coverage.

## Manager Approval

Managers review leave requests based on staffing needs, role coverage, and applicable policy. Managers should respond within a reasonable timeframe and document the approval or denial in the approved system.

## Documentation

Some leave types may require supporting documentation. Staff should provide only the documentation requested by HR and should avoid sharing unnecessary private details with managers or coworkers.

## Medical and Private Information

Medical details, family circumstances, and other private information should be handled by HR through approved confidential channels. Managers should not request more personal information than needed to support scheduling and policy compliance.

## HR Contact Process

Staff who have questions about leave eligibility, documentation, or private circumstances should contact HR directly. HR will provide guidance on what information is required and how it should be submitted.
