# Donation Receipt Process

## Purpose

Community Support Organization uses a consistent donation receipt process to support accurate records, donor trust, and careful handling of donor information.

## Verify Donor Record

Before creating a receipt, staff confirm that the donor record is complete and accurate. Staff should check the donor name, preferred contact method, donation date, and any receipt preferences recorded in the internal system.

## Confirm Donation Amount

The donation amount must match the payment record. If there is a mismatch between the donor record and the payment record, staff should pause the receipt process and ask the finance contact to review the issue.

## Generate Receipt

Receipts are generated through the approved internal donation system. Staff should not create official receipts in personal documents, personal email accounts, or unapproved tools.

## Review Before Sending

Before sending a receipt, staff review the donor name, donation amount, date, receipt number, and delivery method. A second review may be required for corrected receipts or unusually large donations.

## Correct Errors

If receipt information is wrong, staff should not delete the original record without guidance. The error should be documented, corrected in the approved system, and reviewed by the finance contact before a replacement receipt is sent.

## Store Records Securely

Donation records and receipts must be stored in approved internal systems. Donor information should not be pasted into public AI tools or copied into unapproved spreadsheets.
